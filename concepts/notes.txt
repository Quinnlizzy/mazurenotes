Machine learning - 
This is foundation for an AI system, and is the way we "teach" a 
computer model to make predictions and draw conclusions from data.

Computer vision - 
Capabilities within AI to interpret the world visually.

Natural language processing - 
Capabilities within AI for a computer to interpret written or spoken language, and respond.

Document intelligence - 
Capabilities within AI that deal with managing/processing/using high volumes of form/document data.

Knowledge mining -
Capabilities within AI to extract info from volumes of unstructured data & create searchable knowledge store.

Generative AI - 
Capabilities within AI create original content in natural language, image, code, and more.
